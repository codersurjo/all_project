Ocean Outpost Single

It’s Time To Play At CHUKKA OCEAN OUTPOST, Where Ocean-Loving Thrill Seekers And Cultural Explorers Unite. Your Ocean Ultra Pass Gives You All-Day Access To The Only Seaside Nature-Adventure Park In Jamaica. Enjoy The Many Chill-Out And Leisure Areas, Along With Included Select Non-Motorized Watersports, Your Choice Of Four Tours/Expedition Experiences And Lunch At Gilly’s Ocean-Side Restaurant.

Ocean Outpost Tours:

  • Mountain To Sea Horseback Ride ‘N’ Swim
  • Mountain To Sea ATV Safari
  • Mountain To Sea Dune Buggy Safari
  • Power Snorkel Party Catamaran Cruise

Expedition Experiences:

  • Jungle Outpost Zipline
  • Jungle Outpost River Tubing

Included In Your Pass:

CANCELLATION POLICY
  • Any cancellations made at least forty-eight (48) hours or more shall not attract a cancellation fee.
  • Any cancellation made less than 48 hours but more than 24 hours in advance of the scheduled tour time, will attract fifty percent (50%) penalty.
  • Any cancellation made within twenty-four (24) hours or on the same day that the booking is made will attract one hundred percent (100%) penalty.
  • All cancellations must be submitted in writing to the Company and received by Chukka Caribbean Adventures within the timeframes outlined above to be eligible for a refund.
