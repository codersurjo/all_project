Can you do it dude???

It's SLAMminLadies' oh-so-clever name for our downloadable video matches. Most of them are shot exclusively for our websites and are 12 to 20 minutes long. Unless otherwise specified, they are not filmed from the audience during a live show, they are not 3rd generation copies of somebody's old VHS--We shoot these exclusively for this site! Most of them also are shot from ringside so that you get a good clear view of what's going on! Now, here in 2016, we've even gone 4K with the raw files.
We compress them in MPEG, MP4 or WMV format and they can be played back using most any version of Windows Media Player (though you should upgrade to their latest version for best results), GOM Player, VLC and most other players. They're big files because we care, so these are not good for our friends with dial up connections or dated video cards.
Mac users should search engine around for advice on playback.

I Can't Get My Download Started-What the Hey?

In Edge & Chrome:

Go to the picture page linked in your email, Left Click the photo & put the Password set into the box. WMV files should begin downloading automatically. Right Click inside the player window and choose to Save Video As for MP4 files.

In Firefox:

Recent changes to Firefox may make it difficult for you to choose to save your video as the proper file type. At this time the only solutions may be to install a video download add-on such as Turbo Download Manager. Or: You can open the about:config page via the location/address bar. You can accept the warning and click "I accept the risk!" to continue. Change this setting to this and restart Firefox:
media.play-stand-alone = false
You should then be given the option to save your file when you proceed to the video link. All it does is overide the proprietary html5 player.
Not elegant, but there you go. We used to love Firefox too...
Android users: Should work great with the steps outlined above using long presses.


Apple iPad/Mac users: You'll need to download to an external drive, Dropbox or Cloud to access your downloaded files. There are also apps in the Appstore that will allow you to save directly to the Apple file system that may work for you.

How Come My Video Doesn't Play/Sound Right?

A couple of possible reasons. One is that your player may need a specific codec that it's lacking. If that's the case, simply play the video with your Internet browser open and it should "phone home" for the software that it needs.
The temp cache of your browser may be full also. Click "Tools" in the bar up top, go to "Internet Options" and check things out there.
It's also possible that your download was interrupted or corrupted somehow. Try downloading it again. Delete any incomplete version of the file from your hard drive (including deleteing it from your Recycle Bin). Then, be sure to compare the total size of it with what's listed on the site- that will let you know that you have the complete file.
All videos have an intro and an outro of a "SLAMminLadies" graphic; if you don't see those, something's wrong. We don't sell incomplete matches.
Harsh fact-many ISPs are putting caps on bandwidth usage now and you may need to call yours up and check your sevice status-we can't remotely diagnose your connection.
When all else has failed, please drop us a line at slamminladies123@gmail.com and we'll see what we can do to help.
