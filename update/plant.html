WE EMPLOY
EXPERIENCED DRILLERS
AND LICENSED BLASTERS

The excavation of the diabase rock is done by implementing safe, efficient and cost-effective drilling and blasting. Safety governs our every step in constucting a blast plan specific to every situation.

Our skilful team has gathered a wealth of knowledge about the unique characteristics of the diabase work. We use that background to design shot patterns that balanced the desired breakage outcomes with the cost of achieving them. Our team members have specialised training in *** ensuring the work is completed with professionalism and within the European regulations. We foster good relations with regulatory authorities with early, accurate, and frequent communication.

A HIGHLY PRODUCTIVE AND
EFFICIENT CRUSHING PLANT

Our site has a highly productive and efficient Metso Nordberg crushing plant (installed in 2000), with a capacity of 350 tons per hour, and equally as efficient Telsmith screens. The production efficiency has benefited further from 2019's installation of a new conveyor system from Elkayam Metal Industries. The system has reduced the need for wheeled loader transportation of crushed and screened material to stock piles thus reducing fuel consumption and production time.
